Thank you for using **Yaams Timer**.

**Yaams Timer does not collect, store, use, or share any personal information.**

- No analytics, ads, or tracking tools are used

All app functionality runs entirely on your device.

This app does **not** use any third-party services, SDKs, analytics tools, or advertising frameworks.

## Data Storage
The app does not store personal data locally or remotely.
